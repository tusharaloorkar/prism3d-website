Who we are

Things worth printing.
Designs worth keeping.

Prism3D is a 3D printing brand built around objects that actually mean something — playful toys, charming keychains, statement home décor, miniature models, and novelty bobbleheads, all produced right here on our printers, layer by layer.

We curate and craft our own range of designs — pieces made to sit on your shelf, gift to someone, or just make you smile. Every product leaves our studio finished and ready to enjoy. We're just getting started, and there's a lot more coming.
